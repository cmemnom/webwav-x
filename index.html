OUR PROCESS

Take a look at our simple, seamless process - we call it 'The 4 Ds'.

1. Discover

First we'll hop on a quick call and ask you about your business as well as your goals and requirements to make sure we are on the same wave-length. We'll outline what we plan to do, time-frame and setting up good communication pathways to keep you in the loop consistently throughout the building process.

2. Design

We'll start by building a sitemap of the pages and features to be included, before designing wireframes and visuals which will be shown and discussed with you to ensure we agree upon the direction we're heading in. This also gives you a change to improve, change or add aspects you may prefer.

3. Develop

This is where we bring your website to life! We will add webpages, features and other requirements as discussed with you to make sure the website fully represents your business. We'll also present you with a live link - you'll have the power of checking the site's progress at the tip of your fingers!

4. Deliver

Once the website is built, we will test it together with you to make sure it performs to your expectations. Only when you are 100% satisfied with it, we'll hit "launch" and your site will go live! If you ever have any questions or face any issues afterwards, we'll just be a phone call away and will be happy to help no problem at all!
